Summer & Winter Seasons Around the World

Zone-wise climate impact, demographic shifts, and temperature trends

Demographics & Zones

India: Northern India faces extreme summers and cold winters. Southern India experiences a tropical climate.

Europe: Cold winters in the north, milder Mediterranean weather in the south.

North America: Harsh winters in Canada; subtropical summers in the southern U.S.

Australia: Opposite seasons; summer in December, winter in July.

Africa: The Sahara has blazing summers; central Africa stays tropical.

Extreme Temperature Zones

Coldest: Oymyakon (Russia), Antarctica. Hottest: Death Valley (USA), Lut Desert (Iran).
